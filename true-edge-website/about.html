How it works

A simple lease that turns your property into steady income.

Here's the whole idea in one line: we become your long-term tenant. You lease your home to True Edge, we pay you a fixed rent every month, and we handle everything that happens inside it — the furnishing, the guests, the cleaning, the upkeep. You get the income of a rental without any of the work.

The process

Four steps from conversation to steady rent.

01

We learn about your home

We start with a short conversation and a look at your property to make sure it's a good fit, then agree on a fair monthly rent together.

02

We sign a long-term lease

You sign a straightforward lease with True Edge as your tenant. Your fixed monthly payments begin according to the agreement — no waiting on a guest to book.

03

We furnish, insure & maintain it

We outfit the home for comfortable extended stays, put the right insurance in place, and set up a regular professional cleaning and upkeep schedule.

04

You collect steady rent, hands-off

From there, it's ours to run. You receive the same rent every month while we take care of guests, cleaning, and the day-to-day. Nothing lands on your plate.

Good questions

What owners ask us first.

How is this different from a property manager?

A property manager works for a percentage fee — and you still carry the risk of vacancies, problem tenants, and surprise costs. We're not a manager; we're your tenant. We lease the home and pay you a set rent, so that risk shifts from you to us.

How long is the lease?

We agree on the term up front, and we're looking for a long-term arrangement — typically multiple years — so your income stays steady and predictable.

Is my property insured?

Yes. We keep appropriate insurance in place for the way the home is used, for the full length of the lease, in addition to any coverage you carry as the owner.

What if a guest causes damage?

That's our responsibility, not yours. As the leaseholder we're accountable for the condition of the home and we handle any guest-related issues directly — you deal with us, never with the guests.

Who covers utilities and maintenance?

We take care of the day-to-day running of the home. Exactly how utilities and larger repairs are split is spelled out clearly in the lease, so there are no surprises on either side.

My property isn't in Utah — can you still help?

Quite possibly. We're based in Utah and expanding across the country. Reach out with your property's location and we'll tell you honestly whether we can serve your area.
