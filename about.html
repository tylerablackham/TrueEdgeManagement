How it works

From "where am I staying?" to "I'm home."

Finding a place for a work assignment shouldn't be a project of its own. Tell us where you're headed and we'll handle the rest — so the hardest decision you make is which room to work from.

Your stay, step by step

Four easy steps to a home that's ready for you.

01

Tell us about your assignment

Share where you're going, your dates, and roughly how long you'll be there. The more we know, the better the fit.

02

We arrange the right home

We line up a comfortable, fully-furnished home that suits your location, length of stay, and needs — and walk you through it before anything's confirmed.

03

Confirm your stay

Clear, simple pricing with no guesswork. Once it works for you, we lock in your dates and get the home ready.

04

Move in and settle

Arrive to a clean, stocked, work-ready home. We stay on call for anything you need while you focus on the job.

More than a place to sleep

A home that works as hard as you do.

Long assignments are easier when your home supports your routine — a quiet corner for calls, a kitchen for real meals, and space to actually unwind at the end of the day.

That's the difference between counting down the days in a hotel and genuinely living somewhere while you're there.

Good to know

Questions guests ask us.

How long can I stay?

Our homes are built for extended stays — anywhere from a few weeks to several months. Tell us your dates and we'll work around your assignment.

What's included in the price?

Stays come fully furnished with Wi-Fi, a stocked kitchen, linens, laundry, utilities, and professional cleaning — so the price you're quoted is close to the price you pay, with no scramble for furniture or setup.

Which cities do you cover?

We're based in Utah and arranging homes in more locations as we grow. The best thing to do is tell us where you're headed — if we can host you there, we'll say so right away.

Can I bring a partner or a pet?

Often, yes. Let us know up front who (and what) is coming, and we'll factor it into the home we arrange for you.

How do payments work?

Simple, predictable pricing for the length of your stay, laid out clearly before you commit — no hidden fees or surprises.
